## Task

Wire the `onEndTurn` and `onPlayerMoved` handler methods in `tutorial-mode.js` so they detect when the player is standing at the Earth shrine center at turn end, ready to advance the tutorial.

## Files

- `js/tutorial-mode.js` — add/complete `onEndTurn()` and `onPlayerMoved()` method bodies

## Action

1. Read `js/tutorial-mode.js` fully — note current `onPlayerMoved` (currently no-op) and check if `onEndTurn` exists at all
2. Note the constant for Earth position — likely `EARTH_POS = {q: 0, r: 0}` or similar; confirm exact value
3. Implement `onPlayerMoved(q, r)`:
   - During the Earth Shrine step only, check if `{q, r}` matches `EARTH_POS`
   - If yes, show a hint: "You're at the shrine center! Now End Your Turn to collect stones."
   - This is informational only — the step advances on `onEndTurn`, not on move
4. Implement `onEndTurn()`:
   - If the tutorial is on the Earth Shrine step AND the player pawn is currently at `EARTH_POS`, advance to the next step
   - If the player is NOT at `EARTH_POS`, show a gentle nudge: "Walk to the shrine center first, then End Turn."
   - Do not block the end turn itself — just guide

## Verify

- [ ] `onPlayerMoved` detects `EARTH_POS` and shows the hint message
- [ ] `onEndTurn` advances tutorial when player is at `EARTH_POS`
- [ ] `onEndTurn` shows nudge message when player is not at `EARTH_POS`
- [ ] Both handlers are no-ops when tutorial is not on the Shrine step
- [ ] No regression in existing `onTileRevealed`, `onPlayerTilePlaced` handlers
