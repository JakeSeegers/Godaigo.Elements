# Plan 01-03: Insert Earth Shrine Tutorial Step

**Phase:** 1 — Earth Shrine Wiring
**Requirements:** WIRE-03
**Status:** pending
**Depends on:** 01-02 (handlers must be wired before step is inserted)

---

## Task

Insert a new "Earth Shrine" tutorial step between the current step 4 (Earth scroll found) and step 5 (Open Scrolls Panel), and renumber subsequent steps. The new step spotlights the board and instructs the player to walk to the shrine center and end their turn.

## Files

- `js/tutorial-mode.js` — insert step into the steps array, renumber, add spotlight/blocking config

## Action

1. Read the steps array in `tutorial-mode.js` and identify step index 4 (0-based) — the "Found a Scroll" step
2. Insert a new step object AFTER the Earth scroll reveal step with:
   - `id`: `'earth-shrine'` (or similar)
   - `title`: `"Visit the Earth Shrine"`
   - `text`: `"Walk your pawn to the center of the Earth shrine tile. Once you're there, click **End Turn** to absorb 5 earth stones from the shrine."`
   - `spotlight`: board/hex area (or no specific element — let the player explore)
   - `blocking`: `false` — player must move freely and click End Turn
   - `type`: `'move'` or a new `'end-turn'` type that uses `onEndTurn` to advance
   - Advance trigger: `onEndTurn` (not a Next button) — step auto-advances when handler fires
3. Renumber all subsequent steps in both the array and any step-index references (e.g., `goToStep(5)` calls must become `goToStep(6)`)
4. Update the total step count display if it's hardcoded (search for `15` and update to `16`)

## Verify

- [ ] New step appears between "Found a Scroll" and "Open Scrolls Panel"
- [ ] Step text is clear and instructs both movement AND End Turn
- [ ] Next button is hidden — step only advances via `onEndTurn`
- [ ] All subsequent step numbers are correct (no skipped or duplicate indices)
- [ ] Step count in HUD/display shows 16 (if previously hardcoded as 15)
- [ ] No JS errors when step is active

## Done when

Running the tutorial to step 4 (Earth scroll found) shows the new Shrine step next, and completing it (walk + end turn) advances to the Scrolls Panel step.
